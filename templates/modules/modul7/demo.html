🎨 Demo Mode - Color Space Conversion

Jelajahi konversi model warna menggunakan dataset bawaan. Pilih gambar dan analisis yang ingin Anda lihat.

📚 Tentang Model Warna
RGB: Additive color model (Red, Green, Blue)
XYZ: CIE standard color space (tristimulus values)
LAB: Perceptually uniform (Lightness, a*, b*)
YCrCb: Used in JPEG compression
YUV: Analog TV transmission (PAL/SECAM)
🔧 Implementasi Manual
YIQ: Manual dengan matriks NTSC transformation
HSI: Manual dengan perhitungan trigonometri
HSV: OpenCV built-in conversion
CIE Luv: Alternative perceptual uniform space
Normalisasi: Otomatis untuk visualisasi optimal
🧮 Dasar Matematis
🔢 Transformasi Matriks (YIQ):
[Y]   [0.299  0.587  0.114 ] [R]
[I] = [0.596 -0.275 -0.321 ] [G]
[Q]   [0.212 -0.523  0.311 ] [B]
📐 Formula HSI:
Intensity: I = (R + G + B) / 3
Saturation: S = 1 - 3*min(R,G,B)/(R+G+B)
Hue: H = arccos(...) with conditions
🎯 Aplikasi Praktis
📺 Broadcasting & TV:
  • • YIQ: NTSC (Amerika)
  • • YUV: PAL/SECAM (Eropa)
  • • YCbCr: Digital TV & Video
🎨 Graphics & Design:
  • • HSI/HSV: Color picking
  • • LAB: Color difference measurement
  • • RGB: Display & web graphics
🔬 Scientific & Medical:
  • • XYZ: Colorimetry standard
  • • CIE Luv: Light mixing
  • • LAB: Perceptual uniformity
